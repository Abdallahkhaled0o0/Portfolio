About Me

I am a multidisciplinary engineer with a background in architectural engineering, specializing in urban planning & design. My expertise spans both architectural & urban fields allowing me to deal with various scales of projects ranging from detailed architectural & landscape elements to city-scale planning & regional visions.

In addition to Planning & Design, I have practical experience in construction consultancy, and able to provide support in the technical workshop drawings & construction documentations with structural Considerations. I am also aware of the current Environmental situation, I always consider sustainability & eco-friendly solutions.
